Your Complete Guide

Golf Cart Rentals in Costa Rica: Everything You Need to Know

Golf carts are one of the best ways to explore Costa Rica's beach towns. They're legal on most beach roads, easy to park, and make every day feel like a vacation — even getting to the grocery store is fun.

Are Golf Carts Legal in Costa Rica?

Yes. Street-legal golf carts (LSVs — Low Speed Vehicles) are permitted on most roads in Costa Rica's beach towns. All our carts are fully registered, insured, and meet local road requirements.

Which Beach Towns Are Best for Golf Carts?

  • Tamarindo — Golf cart-friendly streets, beach access, estuary roads
  • Flamingo — Hilly terrain, perfect for cart exploration to Conchal
  • Playas del Coco — Flat beach strip, easy cruising
  • Jacó — Long beachfront boulevard, access to Hermosa
  • Manuel Antonio — Scenic road to national park
  • Potrero — Quiet roads, great for sunset drives

What Do I Need to Rent?

A valid driver's license, a credit card for the deposit, and to be at least 21 years old. No special permit or international license required.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your golf cart in Costa Rica.

Are golf carts street-legal in Costa Rica? +
Yes. All providers in our network operate registered, plated, and insured Low-Speed Vehicles (LSVs) that are legally permitted on most roads in Costa Rica's beach towns including Tamarindo, Flamingo, Jacó, Manuel Antonio, Playas del Coco, and Potrero.
What documents do I need to rent a golf cart? +
You need a valid driver's license from your home country, a credit card for the security deposit, and to be at least 21 years old. An international driving permit is welcome but not required in Costa Rica.
Do you deliver the cart to my hotel? +
Yes! Most providers in our network offer hotel and vacation rental delivery. Just include your hotel name or address in the request form and we'll confirm delivery details with the provider. A small delivery fee may apply depending on your distance.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially during high season (December–April) when demand is highest. Same-day and last-minute bookings are sometimes possible — just message us on WhatsApp and we'll check availability.
Is a deposit required? +
Yes, a refundable security deposit is held on your credit card at pickup. The deposit is fully released upon the cart's safe return in the same condition. We do not charge the deposit unless there is damage.
What happens if the cart breaks down? +
All providers in our network offer roadside assistance for their rentals. If anything happens, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll coordinate with the provider immediately to get you back on the road or arrange a replacement cart.
Can I take the cart on the beach? +
Beach access varies by location. Some beaches in Costa Rica permit vehicle access on the sand (like certain areas in Guanacaste), while others are pedestrian-only. Our team will give you a local map and advise on which areas are permitted when you pick up the cart.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee of one day's rental. Just message us on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out — we're flexible and understand travel plans change.
